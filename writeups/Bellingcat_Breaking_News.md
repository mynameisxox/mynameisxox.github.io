A reverse image search quickly reveals where the photo was taken, leading us to Nepal, and more specifically to [this article](https://republicaorigin.nagariknetwork.com/news/construction-of-overhead-bridge-in-new-baneshwor-completed-two-years-later-than-scheduled-deadline):

![[Pasted image 20260313135406.png]]

This gives us the name of a neighborhood: **New Baneshwor**, located in the city of **Kathmandu**.

We then search for Al Jazeera articles featuring an image of this street in their header, covering events that took place in New Baneshwor / Kathmandu / Nepal.

Searching Google for "New Baneshwor / Kathmandu / Nepal Al Jazeera", one of the first results is [this article](https://www.aljazeera.com/features/2025/9/9/we-want-mass-resignations-nepals-gen-z-anger-explodes-after-19-killed):

![[Pasted image 20260313135624.png]]

The caption under the header image reads: *"Protesters on a Kathmandu street waving the national flag and chanting slogans on Monday Samik Kharel/Al Jazeera"*.

We've most likely found the right article. The last two words of the title *'Topple this government': Nepal's Gen Z protesters demand mass resignations* give us our answer.
